Frequently Asked Questions
Do you have a free plan?

Absolutely. You get one free book and daily report.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. We offer a no questions asked cancelation policy. If you are unhappy with the service, contact us at info@bestsellersrank.me, and we'll cancel your service and give you a prorated refund.

Can I get a refund?

Absolutely. We offer a 7-day no questions asked refund policy. If you are unhappy with the service for any reason, contact us at info@bestsellersrank.me, and we'll give you a full refund right away. After 7 days it's simply a service cancelation and you'll receive a prorated refund.

Can I change plans?

Absolutely. If you choose a more expensive plan we'll charge you for the more expensive plan and extend your service period based on how much time you have remaining on your previous plan.

If you choose a less expensive plan we'll add more time to your service period based on the cost difference between the plans and how much you have remaining on your previous plan.

Can I create a custom plan?

Absolutely. Just contact us at info@bestsellersrank.me, and we'll work something out. We're trying to keep the plans as simple as possible, so we don't want to make things complex by default.

How do I subscribe for another year?

A couple weeks before the end of your service period we'll ask you if you want to extend your service. If you do, awesome! If not your account will simple go dormant, but it will not be deleted. You can come back anytime and start the service again.
